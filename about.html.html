💊 Drug Store Management

Who We Are

We are Group 9, dedicated students working on a Drug Store project that helps customers get essential advice and appropriate medical drugs for their health. Our goal is to make medicine information easy, accessible, and helpful for everyone.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support people by providing high-quality health information, trusted drugs, and friendly guidance. We believe that everyone deserves correct medical support and safe drug usage awareness.

What We Provide

✔ Verified drug information
✔ Price list and expiry updates
✔ Advice for medicine selection
✔ Customer-focused guidance
✔ Online assistance for urgent needs

Our Team

We are a hardworking team of students collaborating to make this project successful. Each member contributes different skills to ensure customer satisfaction and system clarity.
